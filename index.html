Warm Welcome To

DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL PUNE

At Delhi Public School Pune, we are committed to nurturing young minds and shaping them into confident, responsible, and compassionate global citizens. As part of the eminent DPS family, our school amalgamates a rich tradition of academic brilliance with a progressive approach to education. Here, children are stimulated to set their sights high, think analytically, and develop an eternal love for learning. With a comprehensive holistic curriculum, ultra-modern facilities, and a team of steadfast educators, we establish an atmosphere where creativity, curiosity and character thrive. Whether it is through academics, sports, arts, or community engagement, we aim to enable and equip our students to realize their full potential and make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To nurture future-ready individuals by blending academic excellence with Indian values, enhancing creativity and innovation in a sustainable, inclusive and technologically advanced learning environment, and empowering students to lead with integrity, compassion and global awareness.

FACILITIES

At Delhi Public School Pune, education goes beyond academics, developing all-round development in a caring, dynamic environment. Nestled in a scenic, pollution-free campus, the school offers state-of-the-art infrastructure, spacious playgrounds, and extensive facilities that support holistic growth.

Our tyre park and sand play areas offer safe, creative outdoor play opportunities.
